About

Food4All is an online platform that acts as a virtual marketplace for Vendors & Consumers. It is a virtual environment that enables proper Re-distribution of Excess Food that is usually disposed off each day, to deserving hands based on Charity.




Disclaimer

Food4All is not an officially licenced application. This is a part of the final year project of the Engineering students of Govt. Model Engineering College, Ernakulam. Names of existing hotels and orphanages are used only for ease of understanding.
